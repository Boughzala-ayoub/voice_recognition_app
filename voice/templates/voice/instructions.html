Instructions

Here is the list of the things that u can do with this app. The instructions that u can say after 1s of pressing the hit me button located in home page are in parenthesis.
Prononciation is an import factor to the app to understand you.
  • to greet her say (hello)
  • to ask her about her name say (what is your name)
  • to ask about her say (who are you)
  • to ask her how she is doing say (how are you)
  • to ask her about the name of her creator say (who created you)
  • to ask for more informations about her creator say (tell me more about your creator)
  • to say goodbye to her say (bye) or (goodbye) (bye bye)
  • to thank her say (thank you)
  • to ask her to give the time say (what is the time)
  • to ask her to give the date say (what is the date)
  • to ask her to search for the weather in a place say (weather in)+ the name of the place for example "weather in paris" to get the weather in paris
  • to ask her to take you to another site in this list [facebook, google, youtube, linkedin, twitter] say (take me to) + one of the words in the list
  • to search in google for something say (search in google for)+ what u wanna search for , for example "search in google for smartphones" to do a google search on smartphone
